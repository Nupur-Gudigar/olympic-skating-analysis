Put names to the pattern ‚Äî Hanyu proved you can have both. Trusova proved technique alone isn't always enough. Alysa Liu proved the era you skate in matters as much as how you skate.

Artistry keeps you on the podium. Technical difficulty puts you on top of it. When two skaters are equally artistic, the one who lands the harder jumps wins. Every time.

The sport is called figure skating, not figure jumping. But the jumps are what decide the gold.
